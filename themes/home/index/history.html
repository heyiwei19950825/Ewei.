1997年

成立地区文化稽查大队

    The establishment of a regional cultural Inspection Brigade

2001年

地区文化稽查大队更名为眉山市文化稽查大队

    The local cultural inspection team was renamed the Meishan cultural inspection team

2006年

市文化稽查大队更名为眉山市文化市场稽查大队

    The City Cultural Inspection Brigade was renamed the inspection team of Meishan cultural market

2009年

市文化市场稽查支队参照公务员法管理

    Municipal Cultural market inspection team refer to civil service law management

2011年

文化、新闻出版、广电执法职能统一整合，眉山市文化市场稽查支队变更为眉山市文化市场综合执法支队

    Cultural, press and publication, radio and television law enforcement functions are integrated, and meishan city cultural market inspection team is changed into the comprehensive law enforcement detachment of meishan city cultural market
